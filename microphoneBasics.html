Microphone Basics

A microphone is a transducer used for the purpose of converting sound into electricity.

Main Types of Microphones

Dynamic

Simple coil moving in a magnetic field

Condenser

Change in capacitance between two charged plates

Ribbon

A strip of foil moving in a magnetic field
